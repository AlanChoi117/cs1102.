Challenge of Virtual Reality and Metaverse in banking

Security problem

Nowadays, it still not have a perfect security policy to protect personal information. As bank accounts include much personal information, such as identity numbers, bank account passwords, and addresses. When the information has been stolen by hackers, it may lead to financial loss. Therefore, it may undermine customer confidence and step back from Metaverse banking.

Lack of regulations

Further monopolisation of digital spaces

The business model of metaverses is online platforms that use the cloud tool to save different personal information, which aims to put offline to online and create a new world. However, the metaverse's security relies on big technology companies, so they have the power to control the information in the metaverse world. The situation of security of information monopoly exacerbated, leading to beyond legal.

Virtual reality

The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) aims to protect data protection between the characteristics in metaverses, such as personal and third-party who process the personal information. However, it still does not have the full regulation in the metaverse. Metaverses as a virtual world will record all people's information and create another electronic identity for people. The advertiser may use the biometric data to classify the customer. Although users in metaverses are required to cancel the data, most data still exist in third-party advertisements. The regulation difficult to regulate all characteristics in Metaverses.
