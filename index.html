Introduction

Have you ever heard about Virtual Reality and Metaverse? With the advent of the enhancement of technology level, more and more companies begin to adopt digital transformation which boost digital acquisition and usage. As a result, face to face environments is gradually eliminated and transformed into digital world.

But what does it relate to banking in the future?

Recently, Banks paid more attention on the development of Virtual Reality (VR) and Metaverse because they have a great potential to reform financial transaction and banking service. With the help of these techologies, financial transactions could occur without time and distance constraint so as to create customized and distinctive service experience for customers which is called Metaverse Banking . However, although using Virtual Reality (VR) and Metaverse have more benefits, we should not overlook their potential risks especially cybersecurity

Let's take a look about Future Banking!!!
